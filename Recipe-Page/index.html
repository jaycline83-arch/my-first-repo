French Toast

Easy french toast mom used to make!>

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Loaf of Bread
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 Stick of Butter

Instructions

  1. Crack and put eggs in a bowl
  2. Turn stove on med and place butter in pan
  3. Add vanilla to bowl and wisk until blendeed smoothly
  4. Once pan is warm,Dip bread into mixture and place into pan
  5. Cook till lightly Brown
